Daily Brief

2026-05-01 · mtime

A morning-paper printer. Pulls weather, stocks, RSS headlines, Hacker News with
LLM-curated comment pull-quotes, dedicated AI / Anthropic section, Reddit, today's
Google Calendar, and a Gmail digest; renders a two-column XeLaTeX newspaper with
inline QR codes for every source link; sends to your AirPrint laser at 8am.

Designed to live next to the other exporters on the Synology NAS and use the
same Container Manager API for deploys.

OUTPUT
- 2 sheets, double-sided (~3 pages of dense content + a clean back).
- Two-column body, masthead with date and weather, full-width indices band.
- Sections: Headlines · Hacker News · AI/Anthropic · Reddit · Markets · Calendar · Inbox · Weather.
- Every linkable item has a small inline QR code so you can scan from paper straight to the source on your phone.

STACK
- Python 3.11 orchestrator with a schedule-based cron loop and a tiny Flask API on :9210.
- XeLaTeX for layout (TeX Gyre Pagella body, Heros sans, Cursor mono).
- WeasyPrint dropped in favor of LaTeX — better typography, real optical column balancing, native QR.
- CUPS inside the container so `lp` Just Works against the AirPrint queue.
