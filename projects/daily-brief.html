Daily Brief

2026-05-02 · mtime

A morning-paper printer. Pulls weather, stocks, RSS headlines, Hacker News with
LLM-curated comment pull-quotes, dedicated AI / Anthropic section, Reddit, today's
Google Calendar, and a Gmail digest; renders a two-column XeLaTeX newspaper with
inline QR codes for every source link; sends to your AirPrint laser at 8am.

Designed to live next to the other exporters on the Synology NAS and use the
same Container Manager API for deploys.

## Output shape

- 2 sheets, double-sided (≈3 pages of dense content + a clean back).
- Two-column body, masthead with date and weather, full-width indices band.
- Sections: Headlines · Hacker News · AI / Anthropic · Reddit · Markets ·
  Calendar · Inbox · Weather.
- Every linkable item has a small inline QR code so you can scan from paper
  straight to the source on your phone.

Sample preview is rendered with realistic mock data — see `outputs/`.

## Stack

- **Python 3.11** orchestrator with a `schedule`-based cron loop and a tiny
  Flask API on `:9210` for ad-hoc triggers and OAuth.
- **XeLaTeX** for layout (TeX Gyre Pagella body, Heros sans, Cursor mono).
  Native multi-column, microtype, qrcode pkg, tcolorbox.
- **WeasyPrint dropped** in favour of LaTeX — better typography, real
  optical column balancing, native QR codes.
- **CUPS** inside the container so `lp` Just Works against your AirPrint
  printer (queue auto-registered from `$PRINTER_URI` at startup).
