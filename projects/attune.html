Attune

2026-04-30 · git · https://github.com/jsharf/attune

A live focus coach. Plays adaptive music with breath cues, watches for fatigue
signals, and steps in with short TTS interventions when you start to drift —
or holds back entirely in music-only mode. BreathNudge (a smaller breath-nudge
spinout) lives as a subdir in this repo.

Recent work has been a UX pass on the live-coach side: music-only gating across
all interventions, a fix for the dismiss → fatigue-still-high → infinite-reprompt
loop, a TTS rate cap, and killing skip-shunt.
